My Goals And Aspirations

In the future, my aspirations are deeply rooted in the tech industry. My foremost goal is to secure my first job in this dynamic field, a crucial milestone that marks the beginning of a fulfilling journey. I envision someone taking a chance on me, recognizing the dedication and quality evident in my work and the unwavering passion fueling my projects. I aspire to forge a career that knows no bounds, offering me limitless opportunities for growth, learning, and innovation on a daily basis. Above all, I yearn to be an integral part of a collaborative team, united by a shared ardor for crafting remarkable websites, where our collective enthusiasm and expertise converge to create something truly exceptional.

Overcoming Obstacles

Overcoming the formidable specters of fear and doubt on my journey through the tech industry was a transformative experience. I pushed forward relentlessly, even when I grappled with feelings of inadequacy and self-doubt, determined to prove my worth. Opting for the self-taught route, I embarked on a path that demanded unwavering patience, forcing me to let go of my ego and recognize that learning is a humbling process. I learned to trust the journey, embracing the wisdom that progress often involves stumbling blocks. My commitment to self-improvement, perseverance, and resilience has not only equipped me with technical skills but has also fostered a mindset that thrives in the face of challenges. These qualities, honed through the crucible of self-guided learning, make me a valuable asset to any team, where I can bring not only technical expertise but also the tenacity to navigate and conquer the ever-evolving tech landscape.

My Story

Born and raised in Saint Louis, Missouri, I embarked on a journey that led me from my hometown to Columbia, Missouri, where I pursued higher education at the University of Missouri. After a year at Mizzou, I found myself seeking a path that truly resonated with my passion. Opting to step away from formal education, I delved into the working world, all the while yearning for that spark of inspiration. It wasn't until a serendipitous encounter during my travels in Europe that I discovered the world of web development. My friend's contagious enthusiasm for her own journey ignited a dormant curiosity within me. However, as life regained its grip, the memory slipped away.

Months later, the memory resurfaced, and I decided to take the plunge into web development. Beginning with YouTube tutorials, I initially felt daunted by my lack of a Computer Science degree. Yet, witnessing self-taught individuals thrive in the field inspired me to forge ahead. I dived into "freeCodeCamp.org," completing web development and Javascript sections to earn certificates. What began as a challenge swiftly transformed into an infatuation. Hours spent in coffee shops and libraries honing HTML, CSS, and Javascript skills became my norm. Coding awakened a passion unlike any other, infusing my mornings with purpose and excitement. The allure of creativity merged seamlessly with practical problem-solving, setting me on a new path.

As I earned my certifications, I embarked on personal projects, culminating in the creation of this very portfolio. Every step has been a joy, from the humbling beginnings to the triumphs of mastering new concepts. The journey has been nothing short of invigorating, and I stand at the cusp of what's to come, energized and excited for the future.
